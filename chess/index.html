Atomic

    Rules

  1. All normal chess rules apply
  2. When a piece is captured, an explosion occurs on the square where the piece is captured. This blows up:
    - the captured piece
    - the capturing piece
    - all pieces within a one-square radius
  3. The game ends by checkmate, or by the explosion of the opponent's king
  4. Suicide (i.e. a move causing your own king's explosion) is not allowed
