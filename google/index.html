Why Google?

Let's change lives

In middle school, I was asked to give a presentation on my dream job. Back then, I wanted to be a writer. Consequently, as a 6th grader, I scoured the Google Jobs site, and decided that I wanted to be a technical writer.

Looking back, it seems strange that I would search for a writing job at a company like Google. I remember my reasons for choosing the job: a creative, interactive, and innovative atmosphere, and highly motivated peers.

While I do still admire these qualities, I've come to realize that I chose Google because I wanted to make an impact, the way Google has impacted me. Google provides the most versatile, powerful, and incredibly "simple" services, without which so many aspects of my life would be compromised.

So, why Google? Because I want to help create new tools that streamline daily processes, and contribute to the extraordinary impact Google makes on everyday lives.
