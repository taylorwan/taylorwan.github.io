Instructions
  1. Please input your name, gender, weight and number of hours you are planning to drink for. The application will calculate your alcohol tolerance according to the information you provided.
  2. Choose the different alcoholic drinks from the sidebar. Keep in mind the percentage of alcohol in each drink by hovering over the drink.
  3. Manipulate the number of drinks by using the + and - buttons on the screen to add and remove drinks.
  4. Keep in mind the percentages and your tolerance as you are adding drinks and the color-coded body on the screen. You are advised to keep the alcohol percentage in your body between the green and orange range.
  5. Once you are done with your choices, the application will visually represent what happens to your brain, heart, liver and bones with drinking.
  6. After finishing your choices, you are directed to a Health Report page that translates your alcohol choices from a health perspective and illustrates the consequences of your choices over time.
  7. Finally, the user is directed to a Test page that prompts the user to answer a couple of questions about the Dos, Don'ts and consequences of drinking in a social scene.
